The diagnosis

The system isn't broken.
It's stabilising.

A stall is not a failure. It is a behavioural substitution — the system doing something observable instead of something harder. Every behaviour that looks like dysfunction makes sense from inside the actor's constraints. When stalls reinforce each other, they form a stack — which is why your cluster works hard and goes nowhere.
