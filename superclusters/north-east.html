North East Combined Authority

Newcastle, United Kingdom Supercluster 6 clusters

North East Combined Authority's innovation footprint draws £4.17bn of UKRI lead-led funding across 4,022 grants spanning 6 active clusters, with North East Life Sciences (33%) the largest single cluster and Newcastle University (35%) the dominant regional anchor by UKRI £.

The region shows medium-confidence "Validation–Coordination" stabilisation stacks at ecosystem grain — validation activity recurs through programmes and partnerships without the testing-and-narrowing motion that converts capability into market position.
