Snapshot
requested.

Andrew will be in touch within one working day to confirm your Cluster Snapshot and send the next steps.

What happens next
01
Andrew confirms your cluster scope and sends the ClusterOS Diagnostic Instrument — the actor questionnaire you'll distribute across your cluster.
02
You distribute the questionnaire to founders, anchors, researchers, and stewards. Responses come back to us directly.
03
We run the public data pipeline alongside the questionnaire responses. 4–5 weeks from instrument distribution.
04
You receive the full Snapshot — stall identification, stack analysis, structural resemblances, leverage hypotheses. With explicit confidence levels throughout.

What happens next
01
Andrew reviews your request and reaches out to arrange a 30-minute scoping call.
02
The scoping call covers what you see, what you don't, and what the board is asking for. This calibrates the diagnostic before it runs.
03
Andrew scopes the engagement and gives you a clear timeline and price. No obligation at this stage.
04
If it's the right fit, you'll receive a proposal within a few days of the call.
