Devon and Torbay Combined County Authority

Exeter, United Kingdom Supercluster 5 clusters

Devon and Torbay Combined County Authority's innovation footprint draws £2.24bn of UKRI lead-led funding across 2,680 grants spanning 5 active clusters, with Devon and Torbay Clean Energy (41%) the largest single cluster and Exeter (44%) the dominant regional anchor by UKRI £. 34 Companies House-traced spin-outs region-wide translate to £66m UKRI per spin-out.

The region shows high-confidence "Validation–Coordination" stabilisation stacks at ecosystem grain — validation activity recurs through programmes and partnerships without the testing-and-narrowing motion that converts capability into market position.
